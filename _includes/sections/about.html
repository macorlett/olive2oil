About us

Olive2oil is a premium, award winning Extra Virgin Olive Oil (EVOO). It is produced by the careful blending of oil from the olives from several varieties of olive tress.The trees are predominately of the Italian origin and grown in the fertile soils of Tauherenikau in the Southern Wairarapa. Olive2oil carries the Olives New Zealand OliveMark certifying that it meets the bottling, labeling, chemical and sensory criteria of EVOO. These criteria are based on international Olive Council standards for EVOO.
